Most people who fall sick with COVID-19 will experience mild to moderate symptoms and recover without special treatment. he virus that causes COVID-19 is mainly transmitted through droplets generated when an infected person coughs, sneezes, or exhales. These droplets are too heavy to hang in the air, and quickly fall on floors or surfaces.
